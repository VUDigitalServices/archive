Before you create a Draft Course, here are a couple of things you might need to know.

P2PU is a little different

P2PU is different from a traditional university. Our core values are openness, community and peer learning - and they inform everything we do.

Starting a learning project with P2PU requires some work and responsibilities:
  • setting expectations for the group: what is your experience level, how much time will the project take, what are participants expected to bring to the table?
  • managing the sign-up process, reviewing applications, and communicating with applicants,
  • initiating introductions and discussions,
  • scheduling and hosting meetings,
  • tracking the overall progress, and reflecting on improvements.

Questions? No worries - we are here to help!

Questions? No worries - we are here to help! You can ask general questions on qa.p2pu.org and the whole community will chime in. You can also engage in more detailed discussions at the P2PU Help Desk.
