I am not sure how this happened, but it appears we ended up with two different Elluminate groups Chris!!

Our discussion included Geoffrey, George, Monica, Kristin, and me. We talked about the competencies frameworks and its history/future at MSU. We also talked about which ones each of us might work on.

I personally think I will focus on Civic Engagement and work on improving my skills at various ways of managing civic engagement through local accounts of wellbeing work, citizen circles work, and whatever else.

I wonder if everyone in this course could be rallied around different ways we want to "show the love" and for whom?
