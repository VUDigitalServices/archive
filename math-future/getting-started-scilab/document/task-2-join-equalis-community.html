Task 2: Join Equalis community

Wed, 2011-05-04 06:03

Equalis is a vibrant online Math community. They provide a full rnage of Scilab support and membership to the community is free.

If you are not already a member, join now. You can join a number of Groups within the Equalis community, including Scilab. Equalis website is at http://www.equalis.com. Join the Scilab group and watch the recorded Scilab webinar.
