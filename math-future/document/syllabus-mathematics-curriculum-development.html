  • Week 6 February 21-27 Exercises, problems, models

  • Week 9 March 14-20 Fundamental ideas of calculus
  • Week 10 March 21 - 27 If you can teach it to a five-year-old...
  • Week 11 March 28 - April 3 Online communities 202 - "organizing your digital self"
  • Week 12 April 4-10 Special topic of your choice, Part 1
  • Week 13 April 11-17 Special topic of your choice, Part 2
  • Week 14 April 18-24 The crossroads
