Summary

Have you ever wondered if people are really rational?  Do you wonder how individual decisions relate to broader societal interests and market phenomena?  Would you like to help yourself and others make better decisions?

We will explore concepts from the field of behavioral economics and apply what we learn in areas related to our personal interests.
