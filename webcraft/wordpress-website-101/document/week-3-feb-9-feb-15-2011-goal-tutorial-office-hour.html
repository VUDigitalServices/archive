Week 3 (Feb 9 - Feb 15 2011) Goal + Tutorial + Office Hour

Sat, 2011-02-12 12:08
Hello Everybody.

Here is our task for the week 3. (http://seatomato.com/p2pu/week-3-agenda/)
1. Understand how to create page template: http://seatomato.com/p2pu/template-for-specific-pages/
2. After you finish the tutorials above, create a page template in your twentyten child theme (or in any theme you use).
Publish a page using your own template, and share in your page what you were trying to achieve in the template. 
For example, name the page template "No-sidebar-no-comments-black-background" and make this template with no sidebar, no comments, and with black background. Create a page using your own template. If you want to incorporate other idea into the template, please do so.
3. To achieve that, read the following page. these pages along with #1 Tutorials will be useful for you to create custom templates.  
Please do take a look at them and utilize them for your page template.
