Tomato Sea

Hi Julie!

For Webhosting -
I honestly can't say much. but it seems like hostmonster is getting good review for the price range.
http://bestwebhosting.org/reviews/hostmonster-review
I say maybe you can try? You can also talk to your peers and ask their opinion.

For FTP -
I tried out Firezilla before and I think it was pretty good. I found the UI a little bit busy but it wasn't a major concern for me. it's free! so why not. :)
I'm using Cyberduck. It's also free. I think Cyberduck has more simpler UI. I feel it's a bit slow but it could be my machine too. :p
You can take a look at this link for more options:
http://www.makeuseof.com/tag/8-free-ftp-clients-for-mac/

For Text Editor -
I just tried out TextWrangler on my machine. I think It looks pretty decent. It highlights the keyword in different colors and makes it easy to read the code. no design view. but it's a free text editor so..

Open for other suggestions!
