I agree with all.Tokbox was really good and allowed for a great discussion. Great find!

In some ways, I thought the experience was even better than a classroom - in the sense that I could see all my classmates face-to-face and observe/ follow their reactions. In a traditional class where we sit facing the facilitator or even if in a circle, you can only directly see very few people.

I also liked the gestures that we used. Raising one's hands to ask a question/ interrupt. Thumbs up sign to indicate approval.

There were very few technical glitches and most of them were at the user's ends - so should be fixed by the second class.

Lastly, it was great to interact with such a diverse and great group of people.
