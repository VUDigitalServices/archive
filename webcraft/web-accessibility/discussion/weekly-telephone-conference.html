Weekly telephone conference

Martin Kliehm on Wed, 2010-09-22 15:51
Go back to: General discussion

Today is Wednesday, but there won't be a telephone conference call today. Let's start next week, 29 September 2010 at 5:00 PM UTC/GMT.

The reason is I'm still struggling to find a free, accessible, browser-based tool for one-to-many sharing of my screen for an initial presentation. Skype allows only 1:1 screen-sharing, and Tokbox, a tool that has been recommended by P2PU, isn't free anymore for scheduled meetings. Also Tokbox is based on Flash and I doubt it's keyboard accessible.

An easy alternative would be using Skype for audio and as a chat backchannel, while previously uploading presentations to Slideshare. That means you'd have to click manually through a couple of slides while I give an initial short talk setting-up the topic. After that we enter discussion and address your feedback from your weekly homework assignment. On the other hand keeping slides on slideshare is more convenient if you miss a telcon.

What do you think? Tokbox or Skype/Slideshare? Do you have recommendations for screen-sharing tools? How do the other courses address that issue? If you have a disability, would any of those solutions work for you?

Cheers,
  Martin
