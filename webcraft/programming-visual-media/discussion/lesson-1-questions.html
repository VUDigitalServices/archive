You can access your own Sketchbook from the link in the application header (assuming you're logged in to http://studio.sketchpad.cc . You can someone else's sketchbook by clicking on their name in any of the lists of sketches. If you have some sketches that you'd like to make more visible to others, use the "move to my public portfolio" option in the action menu next to a sketch list in your Sketchbook. This will make it show up in the Gallery, too: http://studio.sketchpad.cc/sp/padlist/all-portfolio-sketches
Hope that helps!
