Editing the Map with Potlatch 2
 
Once you register an account, use https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/mp/account to set Preferred Editor to Potlatch 2, then click Save Changes at the bottom of the page Now as you explore OpenStreetMap, you can click the "Edit" tab to make changes.
