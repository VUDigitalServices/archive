Tue, 2010-12-07 16:23

We will nominally cover in order the material in the first eight chapters of the Rebecca Murphey's ebook "jQuery Fundamentals" and the bulk of the material in Ben Nadel's video "An Intensive Exploration of jQuery."

Rebecca Murphey's jQuery Fundamentals (Creative Commons Licensed ebook)
http://jqfundamentals.com/book/book.html

Ben Nadel's An Intensive Exploration of jQuery (Video)
http://www.bennadel.com/resources/presentations/jquery/video/index.htm

  • Week 1
    • Introductions
    • Resources and Tools
    • A Short History of jQuery
    • JavaScript Review with emphasis on constructs and idioms used with jQuery
    • Read: Chapters 1 and 2 of jQuery Fundamentals
    • Watch: Sections 1-4 of jQuery Exploration Video
  • Week 2
    • What is Dollar?
    • jQuery Basics
    • CSS Selectors
    • DOM Maniplulation
    • Read: Chapter 3 of jQuery Fundamentals
    • Watch: Sections 5-10 of jQuery Exploration Video
  • Week 3
    • Chaining
    • Traversal
    • Utility Methods
    • Read: Chapters 4 of jQuery Fundamentals
    • Watch: Sections 11-17 of jQuery Exploration Video
  • Week 4
    • Events
    • Animation
    • Read: Chapters 5 and 6 of jQuery Fundamentals
    • Watch: Sections 18-19 of jQuery Exploration Video
  • Week 5
    • Ajax
    • $.load, $.get, $.post, $.ajax Methods
    • Read: Chapter 7 of jQuery Fundamentals
    • Watch: Sections 20-21 of jQuery Exploration Video
  • Week 6
    • Plugins
    • Read: Chapter 8 of jQuery Fundamentals
    • Watch: Sections 22-26 of jQuery Exploration Video
