HTML & CSS From The Beginning - Week 1

Submitted by Jamie Curle on Mon, 2011-05-09 10:07

Good News Everyone,

The first week's tasks on the inaugural semester of HTML & CSS from the Beginning have uploading into the course documents area of the class. 

This week is very much about warming up our learning muscles and getting to know everyone. You've been split into groups because of the huge size of the class, but all of this is explained in the first week's tasks.    

This is the first time that I've ran this version of the course online so feedback is welcomed on my blog post about week 1. However, please don't use it as your first port of call to ask for help if you're stuck. Feedback yes, help no.

I've outlined how you should go about solving your learning problems in the documentation for the week which can be found under notes and guidance at the bottom of the document

I'm going to try my best to be available in IRC throughout the day, (be aware that I'm at work at the same time) and I'd encourage anyone who is able to 'hang out' in the IRC channel to do so. It'll be a great way to get to know each other and help each other learn.

I'm still not 100% sure how I'm going to get round all of your blogs, but from 12pm GMT on Sunday the 15th of May I'll leave feedback for everyone who has completed the tasks. 

If your blog address has changed since you submitted your application please let me know asap so I can update my records to prevent me from missing your work.

Have fun with week 1.

Cheers, 

Jamie.

p.s - See you in IRC.
