Fri, 2010-09-17 19:50

Power vs. Force, David Hawkins. This book creates a measurement system for states of consciousness ranging from shame, anger, and pride to altruism, compassion, and enligtenment. Also deals with "strange attractors." Hawkins states that there are two main states that help us increase our consciousness: moving from "blame" to "responsibility" (at a level of 200 points), and  reaching a lifestyle of "accepting love and non-judgemental forgiveness" (500 points on the scale).(Hawkinds, p. 198)
