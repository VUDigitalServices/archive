Peer to Peer Review and links to course blogs

Josmas Flores on Fri, 2011-01-28 19:14
Go back to: General discussion
Hi guys, 
 
as explained before, the idea of the Peer to Peer review in this course is that each of us receives feedback about their blog posts from at least 2 other people every week.
 
Blog posts should relate your learning experiences (or frustrations!) during the week. You can write as many as you want, and they are expected one day after the weekly meeting.
For the non-English speakers (me included!) I would ask if you could write at least one of your posts in English (or translate your original post) so that other people can review it.
 
I was going to set a roster for reviewing purposes but I'm having second thoughts about it. Mainly because we will split very shortly into smaller groups, so it wouldn't make much sense to have to review something that you have not worked on (or are even interested on). The second reason is that there is a number of people following the course without being members of the course (if you guys out there want to be included in this list, please add your blog link to this thread).
 
Please let me know if you guys prefer a roster and I'll come up with one.
