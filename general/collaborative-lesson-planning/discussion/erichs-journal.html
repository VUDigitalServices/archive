Erich's Journal

Erich Smith on Fri, 2011-01-28 05:29
Go back to: General discussion

My name is Erich Smith and I am in the mist of developing a "blended" classroom for adult learners with the end goal of turning motivated ABE (Adult Basic Ed) learners into motivated online learners (to expand their horizons; see the viability in online courses, certificates, and degrees; non-degree focused continuing education/life-long learning options).

This course I believe will entice me to "lower my guard" and develop openness around ideas shared by others via their own experiences.  I believe this will lead to crucial input . . . not only with lesson planning . . . but classroom application of concepts and ideas found behind every lesson plan.
