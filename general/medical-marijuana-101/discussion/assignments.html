1) What is cloning? Taking off springs from a mother plant, which in turn can become mother themselves. They are exact replicas of the mother. Also, it is a way to save or to cut growing time down!

2) Name 3 ways to sprout seeds. Put seeds in a clear plastic or glass cup filled about half way with room temp. bottled water. Put in a dark place. Soaking will soften hulls and promote germination. After about three days the hull will split and a white tip will emerge. When the tip is about 1/2" it is ready to plant. Can place sprouts in pete pods and treat like seedlings. Or you can just put the seed in non-fertilized potting soil. I place them about an inch deep, this strengthens the roots, and makes for stronger stocks.

4. How many hours of light should clones have while rooting? 24 hours

1. What is cloning?
Cloning consists in taking a cutting from a source plant and grow it apart. The new plant will have exactly the same characteristics than the source plant, the mother. This procedure is much faster than seeding.

2. Put the seeds in a glass of bottled water, they may sink or not but it doesn’t care anyway. Then put the glass in a closed cupboard so it is dark. After three they might be ready remove. They are ready when the sprout is half an inch long.

3. 3 safe ways to order seeds.
As they are not illegal in Spain, you can just buy them in growshops (specialized shops dedicated to the growth of marihuana where you can find everything you need). It is possible to order them on the Internet (www.sensiseeds.com) without any kind of problem. But actually, the easiest and cheapest way to get them is to ask for them to someone who has planted before.

4. How many hours of light should clones have while rooting?
24 hours a day.

1. What does Cannabis do during the vegetative stage?
It is the marijuana maximum growth cycle. In this stage leaf tissue is created, the more leaf surface, the more the plant can grow. This stage takes place from the third to the fifth month when grown naturally.

2. How does Cannabis switch from Veg to Flower?
The plant has a flowering hormone that is present in all stages growth (so we must be careful with that) and is sensitive to light. Consequently as the vegetative stage takes places when the day is longer, the plant will switch to flower stage when daylight decreases.

3. What are 3 grow mediums to choose from?
Soil, hidroponic, and rockwool.

4. How many hours of light should Cannabis have while flowering?
12 hours of light, half day light and half night.
