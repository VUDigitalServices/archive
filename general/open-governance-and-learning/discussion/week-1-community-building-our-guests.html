Week 1 - Community Building - Our Guests

Marisa Ponti on Fri, 2011-01-21 18:55
Go back to: General discussion

Hello All:

we can benefit from the participation of three guests:

Week 2: Michel Bauwens - founder of the P2PU foundation. You can learn more about him here: http://p2pfoundation.net/Michel_Bauwens

Week 3: Phillip Schmidt - founder of P2PU

Week 6:  Wouter Tebbens - founder of the Free Knowledge Institute (FKI) and the Free Technology Academy (FTA)

These guests are all *stars* in the world of p2p and free knowledge, so we are really happy that they accepted our invitation to contribute to this course.

Synchronous meetings with them would be ideal but perhaps difficult to setup because people are spread across time zones.

An alternative option can be to host an asynchronous meeting space - for one or two days during the specific week - where you  log in at different times, share documents and post comments. This meeting space can be this forum, for example. This tool can do the job because supporting discussion-like activities it is what we need, I guess.

However, it would be a good thing to understand better how we want to "use" :-) our guests.

For example: during week 2 we learn a bit about the difference between governance and government. Perhaps we could discuss the concepts with Michel - I am sure he has tons of things to say about this.

Comments and suggestions are welcome
